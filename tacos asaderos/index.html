About Us

We are family owned and operated. Family is very important to us - we treat you like family when you come in.

Together as a family, we have over 40 years experience in TJ style food. Working in TJ and San Diego in various mexican restaurants, taco shops, and taquerias we have perfected the traditional TJ taco style.

We put our love in every food item we make. All our produce is locally sourced and we make everything from scratch daily. We use our own authentic recipes.
